# PRODUCT.md — Raunak Singh, portfolio

register: brand

## Product purpose

A personal portfolio for Raunak Singh, an SDE at Fynd working on the Zenith
Intelligence Platform. The site has one job: in the first 20 seconds, convince a
sharp engineering reader (recruiter, eng manager, fellow builder) that Raunak is
a serious full-stack and AI-native engineer with real shipped work, not a
template. Design IS the product here.

## Users

- **Engineering recruiters and hiring managers** scanning fast, numb to generic
  dev portfolios. They want proof of depth and ownership, quickly.
- **Fellow engineers / founders** who land from his LinkedIn AI-internals series
  or GitHub and want to see what he builds.
- **Future collaborators** evaluating his side projects (Vordi, Bookmarx).

## Tone

Confident, precise, technically literate. Lets specifics do the bragging
(36k recovered events, idempotent per-table mutexes, a 70% retry rate killed by
structured outputs). Never breathless, never buzzword soup. Dry wit allowed.
First person, but earned.

## Anti-references (what to never look like)

- Dark-mode AI-bro portfolio with a neon-purple glow, particles, and a fake
  hacker terminal. This is the first-order category reflex; avoid entirely.
- The "Hi, I'm X 👋, a passionate developer" hero.
- Identical icon-heading-text card grids. Hero-metric stat templates.
- Buzzword clouds with no evidence behind them.

## Strategic principles

1. **Evidence over adjectives.** Every claim carries a number, a system name, or
   a concrete decision.
2. **Show the AI usage honestly.** Raunak reverse-engineers how AI agents store
   context and builds tooling on top. That is the through-line, not a sidebar.
3. **The war-stories are the centerpiece.** Bookmarx and Vordi get real
   engineering-log treatment: problem → root cause → fix.
4. **Editorial, not generic.** Read like a well-typeset engineering dossier.
